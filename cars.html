What we Build

CR800

Since we had no earlier experience of manufacturing a race car, we first decided to get that out of the way by coming up with a prototype vehicle. We wanted to avoid the situation of turning up at the competition with the first car we collectively built. Thus came into existence the CR800, our first prototype. This was a car which was built from whatever scrap material we could find in and around the University.The team went down to brass tacks and we entered the vast ocean that is motorsport engineering!
The CR800 was the first big success we had and we unveiled it publically at SRM University’s biggest national cultural fest ‘Milan 2011’ in March in front of 2000+ people. We generated a lot of interest during this event post the launch.
